Title: Senior Machine Learning Scientist

Company Overview:
DataMind Labs develops advanced AI systems for recommendation, personalization, and decision automation.

Responsibilities:
- Lead end-to-end ML projects from data exploration to deployment and A/B testing.
- Develop and optimize models using deep learning, gradient boosting, and graph-based techniques.
- Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to model explainability and fairness initiatives.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with product and engineering teams to define AI-driven features.
- Publish internal whitepapers and share learnings with the wider AI community.

Requirements:
- Ph.D. or M.S. in Machine Learning, Statistics, or related quantitative field.
- 5+ years of experience developing and deploying ML models at scale.
- Expertise in PyTorch, TensorFlow, and data science libraries (NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn).
- Proficiency in SQL and data pipeline orchestration tools (Airflow, Prefect, or similar).
- Strong background in probability, optimization, and modern neural network architectures.
