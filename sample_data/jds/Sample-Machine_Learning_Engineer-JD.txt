Title: Machine Learning Engineer

Company Overview:
InnovateAI is a fast-growing tech company focused on applying AI and data science to enterprise workflows.

Responsibilities:
- Design, train, and deploy scalable machine learning models for NLP and predictive analytics applications.
- Collaborate with software engineers to integrate ML models into production systems.
- Conduct data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation.
- Implement best practices in model versioning, reproducibility, and monitoring.
- Research emerging ML frameworks and tools to enhance our capabilities.

Requirements:
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field.
- Strong proficiency in Python and frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
- Experience with cloud ML environments (AWS Sagemaker, GCP Vertex AI, or Azure ML).
- Solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, and model evaluation techniques.
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
